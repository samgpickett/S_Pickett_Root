Nendel's Inn

This fancy little restaurant was owned by Bob Harrington and his wife Nancy.

From personal records of employees it appears that the kithen has a number of different head chefs. In spite of this rotation Nendel was infamous for their delcious menu. Nendel's must have closed down in the 1970's because I can find no information about the restaurant past that point. Legend has it that the food is still talked about in 'Stump Town' to this very day.
