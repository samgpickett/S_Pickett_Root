Amelios Cafe

Amelios opened in 1926.

There is little information about Amelio's restaurant. There is a copy of their menu floating around the internet. It has array of classic dishes all written out by hand in brush script. After Amelios closes it was converted into an Italian restaurant called Correnti;s. The place never did well there and didn't last long. Recentley the place was leased to the principals of a Bolivian musical group named Sukay. They restaurant is called Pena PachaMamma.
